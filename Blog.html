On my way to buy a touring bicycle

I got this new hobby after the biking trip to Tien Giang with my friend. The trip was great and I found out that I simply enjoyed the mere experience of cycling.

It gives me a peace of mind moment and makes me realize the beauty around. On the road. During the sunset. Or simply through a rice field.

I stopped thinking about death for a while...

I bought a new cycling helmet just this noon. It is a high quality one (of this producer) and I hope I won't lose it, unlike my motorbike helmet recently.
We bought couple helmets twice and always lost one of each pair. Heartbroken, isn't it? Part of me thinks it's destiny. The other thinks it's bullshit belief! Haha.
I believe in the latter.

Have faith.

Now I will take care of myself better

I know I've been very bad with self-care especially since I was in the best relationship of my life. She was lovely, lively and ... lovely. I could be all day by her side. We would make jokes, all the weird ones, laugh at stupid thoughts and enjoy little things in our lives.

But now she's away.

I should stop thinking about her. Time to be alive again. Renew myself. Find a new goal. Achieve it. Impress myself instead of somebody else.

Because there are still a lot to learn. And you can't while all you do is giving yourself excuses.
